Who am I?

I’m a multimedia designer who loves playing with contrast — between minimalism and nostalgia, polish and personality. My style often draws inspiration from the early 2000s and late 90s. I’m fascinated by the way visual identity can feel both familiar and fresh at the same time.

I focus on creating aesthetics that are bold in concept, clean in execution, and full of intention. Whether I’m working with layout, animation, or image composition, I care deeply about atmosphere — the kind that makes something feel just right, even when it’s a little unexpected. I’m especially drawn to creative direction that uses strong visuals to say something soft.

I like visuals that aren’t afraid to be a little exaggerated, a little nostalgic, or a little different — as long as they’re clear in their message and true to their mood.

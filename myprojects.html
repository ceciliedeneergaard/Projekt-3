Kids clothing collection video ad

In this project, I was inspired by the feeling of being a child again - being playful, free and unfiltered. The ad highlights the clothes through the child's natural movement, letting the unbotheredness of childhood speak louder than the styling of the clothes. The video resembles a camcorder or a home video taping - a choide made to reinforce the theme of childhood nostalgia and authenticity.

MULTIMEDIA DESIGNER WITH
A PASSION FOR


I’m a multimedia designer who loves playing with contrast — between minimalism and nostalgia, polish and personality. My style often draws inspiration from the early 2000s and late ’90s — not just in colors or styling, but in attitude. I’m fascinated by the way visual identity can feel both familiar and fresh at the same time.

I focus on creating aesthetics that are bold in concept, clean in execution, and full of intention. Whether I’m working with layout, animation, or image composition, I care deeply about atmosphere — the kind that makes something feel just right, even when it’s a little unexpected. I’m especially drawn to creative direction that uses strong visuals to say something soft — or uses simplicity to deliver a punch.

I like visuals that aren’t afraid to be a little exaggerated, a little nostalgic, or a little different — as long as they’re clear in their message and true to their mood.

MY PROJECTS

My work explores how visual storytelling can feel refined without losing character. I often experiment with nostalgic references — Y2K energy, dreamy lighting, offbeat poses, or unexpected crops — and combine them with clean compositions and neutral tones. It’s about finding beauty in the details while staying sharp in the overall direction.

Each project is an opportunity to build a small universe — where the styling, rhythm, and tone all work together. I enjoy shaping images that are aesthetically strong but also have a certain feeling behind them: a bit of fun, attitude, or elegance. Whether the focus is a product, a mood, or a brand, I aim to create visuals that are not just beautiful — but distinctive and memorable.
